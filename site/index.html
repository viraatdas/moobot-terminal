moobot.

A Mac trading terminal with an AI research desk.

Moobot Terminal connects to your Robinhood account and puts a fleet of research agents behind your trading. Open a research tab — “NVDA earnings setup”, “uranium miners” — and an agent works the topic continuously: news, filings, price action, sentiment, distilled into a living thesis.

When the evidence supports a trade, the agent files a proposal. Nothing executes without your approval. You are the only one with the trigger.

Install

brew install --cask viraatdas/tap/moobot-terminal

Apple Silicon · requires Claude Code for the research engine · bring your own Robinhood account.

What it does

  • Trade from the terminal portfolio, positions, quotes, and an order ticket over Robinhood — review first, then place
  • Hyper research tabs continuous agent research per topic with a live activity feed and a maintained thesis, not a chat log
  • Trade proposals, human trigger agents propose with thesis and confidence; every order requires your explicit approval
  • Your sources web, SEC EDGAR, market data, social sentiment — configurable per research tab
